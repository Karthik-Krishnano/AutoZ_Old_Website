Team Autoz




MISSION

To design and develop India’s first autonomous car deployable on Indian roads with typical traffic conditions, on the way we wish to give and take help from like-minded organisations.

VISION

Striving to make a change for the betterment of humanity through the extensive use of technology to mimic human intelligence.

ABOUT US

Team AutoZ, is a research & development team of VIT Vellore, driven by a diverse set of undergraduate students having prior experience in industrial projects. We work on developing autonomous vehicles and automated systems. We collaborate and partner with government agencies, businesses and startups to make autonomous and intelligent technology accessible and affordable for all.
