Team AutoZ

Team AutoZ is a research & development team driven by undergraduate B.Tech students of VIT Vellore. The primary aim of our team is to design and develop autonomous ground vehicles and to use our innovation for practical applications of the bot to solve real-world issues, and to work with companies that are keen on trying to bring automation into the market.

We have named our project Pratham, which symbolises our first fully autonomous ground robot. Pratham is capable of performing autonomous navigation under various situations.
